What's new

  • New Music Block

    The school using government funding has recently built a brand new music block which has been used for holding concerts, storing intruments and even hosting music lessons. This has provided students a space in which they can practice their instrumentation and receive their 8th grade in their intrument with ease. Additionally, the classrooms within the block are kitted with each 16 keyboards, one grand piano and an interactive whiteboard in order to boost their learning experience while in school.

  • Brand new cricket pitches

    The massive cricket pitches have recently opened at QE last summer term in order to aid students with their cricketing skills. Thanks to this, not only are the students extremely skilled, but are the best team of cricketers in the county.
